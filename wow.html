WE’VE GOT THE WOW FACTOR!
Dear team,

I am happy to share with you that Hitachi Data Systems has won the Watch Out World (W.O.W.) Special Mention Award by Great Place to Work® Singapore.

This award is special to us as it recognizes our efforts in promoting a friendly work culture. As a company, we truly value our employees and strive for an open environment for the team. It is rewarding to see that the efforts we make result in a working environment that feels like a family. Having our efforts recognized by Great Place to Work® Singapore, and being given this prestigious award for the first time, is a bonus!

The results of the survey show that a high percentage of employees agree that Hitachi Data Systems is a pleasant place to work with friendly team environment, flexible working arrangements and a positive team spirit. Thank you for upholding the Hitachi founding principles of Harmony, Sincerity and Pioneering Spirit and for bringing your best to work every day. Let us continue to work towards a positive workplace in the years to come.
Ravi Rajendran
